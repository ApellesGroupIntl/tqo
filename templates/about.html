Our Story

Founded in 2024, TQO HUB has been revolutionizing the event ticketing industry in Kenya.

We started with a simple mission: to make event discovery and ticket purchasing seamless, secure, and enjoyable for everyone. What began as a small platform has grown into Kenya's premier event ticketing solution, serving thousands of happy customers.

Our team of passionate event enthusiasts works tirelessly to ensure you have access to the best events in your area, with the easiest booking process possible.

Our Mission

To connect people with amazing experiences by providing a seamless platform for event discovery and ticket purchasing, while supporting event organizers with powerful tools to grow their audiences.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading event technology platform, transforming how people discover and experience events while creating meaningful connections between organizers and attendees across the continent.
